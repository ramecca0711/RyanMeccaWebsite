About

An analyst's rigor, a builder's instinct.

I help organizations turn complexity into clarity, and then keep it that way.

For years I've led end-to-end operations work for Fortune 500 companies, building the models, dashboards, and strategies that move the numbers, and teaching the teams that have to live with them.

I started as an analyst deep in Python and Power BI, was the most-utilized resource on my team, and grew into leading client relationships and workstreams as a senior consultant. Today I'm a Business Operations & Growth Manager at Parachute, focused on scaling how the business runs.

My work spans distribution network strategy, throughput and capacity modeling, KPI and BI frameworks, process design, automation, and AI enablement. I'm equally at home in a warehouse mapping a process and in the code that optimizes it. Georgia Tech trained the analyst in me; curiosity and a love of building keep me growing.

Outside of work I'm a cultivator, of plants, community, and good systems. You'll usually find me running, cooking for friends, or exploring Austin.
