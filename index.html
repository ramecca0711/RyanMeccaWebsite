01 / About

I build systems where people and ideas can grow.

I work at the intersection of analytics and operations. My focus is clarity: taking something complicated and making it structured, legible, and ready to act on.

I'm the person who builds the model, maps the process, and sets up the small systems that keep work flowing. It is the same instinct I bring outside the office, whether that means organizing a kitchen before a dinner with friends or building a routine that makes daily life run more smoothly.

I take care of my body through fitness, find inspiration in Austin's food and music, and stay grounded through time in nature. For me, good work and a good life come from the same place: building structure where people and ideas can grow.
