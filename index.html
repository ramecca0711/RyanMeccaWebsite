01 — The Story

I've always been a cultivator.

I love growing things — whether it's plants, strong friendships, or a system that makes life run more smoothly. I'm the friend who organizes the kitchen before we cook together, the one who sets up small rituals that keep a home or a project flowing.

I take care of my body through fitness, find inspiration in Austin's music and food, and stay grounded through time in nature. For me, joy comes from building systems and spaces where people — and ideas — can grow.
